Tank Reports

Water level analytics

Multi-device dashboard live from Firebase Realtime Database. Readings appear only when the level changes—reports highlight gaps, consumption windows, and current state.

Device

Choose a device from your allowlist (Admin). Data is loaded from your Firebase Realtime Database URL (see Admin).

Report range

Water level (%)

Use the Chart control in Report range to switch this view.

Lower distance usually means more water under the sensor.

Longer bars mean more time passed before the next upload (unchanged level).

Largest water drops (consumption)

Each bar is one step between two readings: level fell by this amount, ending at the time on the label.

Largest rises (refill / gain)

Each bar is a level increase between two uploads—often a fill or recovery.

Running totals in this window

Cumulative sum of all level drops (consumed) vs all rises (filled), from the first to the last sample.
